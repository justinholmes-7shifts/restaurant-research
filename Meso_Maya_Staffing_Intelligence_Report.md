## WORKFORCE MANAGEMENT NEEDS ASSESSMENT

### PAIN POINTS MAPPED TO SOLUTION FEATURES:

**1. SCHEDULING OPTIMIZATION NEEDS**

**Evidence:**
- "Long 12 hr shifts" with variable scheduling
- Staff sent home when slow (poor demand forecasting)
- Understaffed during rush hours
- One server for 19-person party (inadequate shift planning)

**Solution Features Needed:**
- Demand-based scheduling algorithms
- Labor forecasting tied to reservations/historical data
- Shift templates for large parties/events
- Real-time schedule adjustments

**2. COMMUNICATION & COORDINATION GAPS**

**Evidence:**
- "Disorganized staff" across multiple locations
- Staff "sitting in booths" while customers wait
- Orders taking 15-20+ minutes to acknowledge
- "No clear systems in place, leadership is nonexistent"

**Solution Features Needed:**
- Team communication tools
- Task assignment/checklists
- Shift notes and handoff protocols
- Manager-to-staff messaging

**Evidence:**
- "Managers don't care if you're sick, you'll get written up"
- Staff sent home early (need accurate hour tracking)
- "Doing the work of 5 people" (workload documentation)

**Solution Features Needed:**
- Mobile time clock
- Attendance tracking with PTO management
- Overtime alerts
- Labor cost tracking by shift/day

**4. SHIFT COVERAGE & SWAPPING**

**Evidence:**
- Understaffing during rushes
- No bartender available (coverage gaps)
- Service breakdowns suggest call-outs not covered

**Solution Features Needed:**
- Shift swap marketplace

**Evidence:**
- 7+ Meso Maya locations

- Inconsistent service quality across locations
- Corporate oversight challenges mentioned in reviews

**Solution Features Needed:**
- Enterprise dashboard for multi-location visibility

- Standardized scheduling templates

**Evidence:**
- "Get a year or two of experience here and move on"

**7. HIRING & ONBOARDING WORKFLOWS**

**Evidence:**
- 44+ active job postings across locations
