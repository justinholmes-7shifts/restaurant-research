## Evidence Tier Classification

After scoring, classify evidence quality:

### TIER 1 - Smoking Gun Evidence
**Characteristics:**
- Legal/compliance documentation (DOL, lawsuits, settlements)
- 5+ employees reporting same specific issue
- Public company acknowledgment of payroll problems
- Customer reviews where staff complained about pay
- Photo/document evidence (leaked pay stubs, timecards)

**Action:** Pursue immediately. Skip ahead in queue.

**Expected conversion rate:** 40-60%

### TIER 2 - Strong Evidence
**Characteristics:**
- 2-4 employees reporting same payroll issue
- Multi-state complexity with documented tip credit differences
- Recent ownership/system changes with employee complaints
- Weekly pay switch + job posting volume
- Multiple independent sources confirming same problem

**Action:** Pursue this week. High priority.

**Expected conversion rate:** 25-40%

### TIER 3 - Moderate Evidence
**Characteristics:**
- 1 employee mention (detailed and recent)
- Operational complexity without direct complaints
- Indirect signals (understaffing driving OT costs)
- Industry-standard problems (tip pooling complexity)

**Action:** Qualify with discovery call before deep pursuit.

**Expected conversion rate:** 10-20%

### TIER 4 - Weak/No Evidence
**Characteristics:**
- No payroll mentions in research
- Only assumptions based on size/industry
- Vague or old mentions
- Only scheduling complaints

**Action:** Deprioritize or skip. Not qualified.

**Expected conversion rate:** <5%
